# Enormous Input Test

You are given **N** integers. Your task is to find how many of them are divisible by **K**.

---

## Input Format

- The first line contains two positive integers **N** and **K**.
- The next **N** lines each contain one positive integer denoted by **Ai**.

---

## Output Format

- Output a single integer denoting how many of the given numbers are divisible by **K**.

---

## Constraints

- `1 ≤ N, K ≤ 10^7`
- `1 ≤ Ai ≤ 10^9`

---

## Sample Input
7 3
1
51
966369
7
9
999996
11
## Sample Output
4

---

## Explanation

The integers divisible by `3` are:
- `51`
- `966369`
- `9`
- `999996`

So, the total count of numbers divisible by `3` is **4**.

---

## More Info
